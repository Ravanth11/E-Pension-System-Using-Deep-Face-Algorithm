FAQ Section

How do I apply for pension benefits?
You can apply for pension benefits by filling out the application form available on our website and submitting it along with the required documents.
What documents do I need to submit with my pension application?
You will need to submit a copy of your identification, proof of age, proof of employment, and any other relevant documents requested in the application form.
How long does it take to process a pension application?
The processing time for a pension application varies depending on various factors, but it usually takes around 4-6 weeks.
Can I apply for pension benefits online?
Yes, you can apply for pension benefits online through our website. Simply fill out the online application form and submit it along with the required documents.
